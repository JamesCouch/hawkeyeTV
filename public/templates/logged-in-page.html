Open DNS Resolver Scanner

This utility allows you submit a subnet to be scanned for Open DNS Resolvers. Open DNS Resolvers are systems that respond to recursive queries for hosts outside their domains. They can be used in DNS Amplification attacks and therefore pose a threat. Please break up any subnets larger than /22 into smaller subnets for accuracy and better response time.
